About Us

Dave's Agency (DA) was founded by Mr. David Goh Sui Leong way back in 1983. Before DA, he started his career as a sales personnel in Borneo Traders Motors (dealer of Mercedes Benz & Mitsubishi) in 1977 at the age of only 19. In 1980, he joined Boustead Sdn Bhd (dealer of Nissan & Suzuki), also as a sales personnel.

After 6 years of experience in the car industry, he ventured out on his own business in 1983. It was during this time where Daveâ€™s Agency was established and began selling used cars, which hit sales of 373 units during that year.

In 1984, Dave's Agency was incorporated and became the authorized dealer of TCY Motors Sdn Bhd (dealer of Nissan) until 1988. In 1989, Dave's Agency Sdn Bhd (DASB) wanted to become more independent and became the sole distributor for Alfa Borneo and Seat in Brunei, which eventually down-sized till late 1996 due to poor market reception.

However, it was during this long downfall that made Mr. David Goh regained his confidence and started expanding DASB which lead to becoming the authorized dealer of Boustead Sdn Bhd selling Nissan and Suzuki new cars. Presently, DASB no longer caters to Nissan and Suzuki automobiles.

In September 2008, DASB resumed its business of buying and selling used cars, ranging from local and imported from Japan and Singapore and in April 2012, DASB became the new car sole distributor of China's famous brands, "Jinbei: and "Zotye", with another brand "Xinkai" coming soon in July 2013.

With years of experience and trust garnered from customers of DASB, it has indeed established itself as a trusted car company, with a customer base of 5,000 in total. Moreover, our head office is currently located at the prime car industrial area in the Capital of the country.
